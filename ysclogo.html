YSC Logo 2012

download logo

Type

Freelance project

Role

Graphic Artist

Software

Objective

Design a logo for the 2012 Young Star Challenge talent show.

Process

With a new talent show competition at the TD Vancouver Chinatown Festival particularly targeting younger competitors of age 5 to 12, a new and playful logo had to be designed. Hence, I offered to help in illustrating a playful logo to enrage kids to participate. I first produced various sketches and researched colour schemes, as well as shapes that signifies child-play. Next, the logo was drawn on Adobe Illustrator and that childish typography has been used in order to relate to their environment.

Result

Since this was a new competition at the festival, there was a large respond at the audition and that chairmans were pleased with the outcome and has invited me to create further illustrations for other events.
